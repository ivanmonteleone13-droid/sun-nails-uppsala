## Why This Business Was Selected

1. **No dedicated website** – Listed only on Hitta.se, Reco.se, and directory sites. Hitta even prompts them to "create a website."
2. **Perfect 5.0 rating** – Strong customer satisfaction on public reviews.
3. **Prime central location** – Stora Torget is highly visible; a website would capture foot traffic and search intent.
4. **Multiple services** – Shoe repair, leather goods, key copying, and retail products — ideal for structured service pages.
5. **High local search value** – "skomakare Uppsala" and "nyckelservice Uppsala" are practical, intent-driven searches.

---

## Opportunities for Improvement

1. **Professional homepage** – Hero, services overview, location on Stora Torget, phone CTA.
2. **Service pages** – Dedicated sections for repair types, key service, and retail products.
3. **Opening hours** – Clear hours on site with note to call ahead.
4. **Google Maps embed** – Help visitors find the shop from search or email links.
5. **Schema markup** – ShoeStore / LocalBusiness structured data.
6. **Review showcase** – Highlight 5.0 rating from public reviews.
7. **Mobile-first design** – Most local searches happen on phones while walking downtown.
8. **Sustainability angle** – Repair vs. replace messaging resonates with eco-conscious customers.
